SPANISH ERA:
FOOD AND CUISINE

Spain's culture is one of the most influential food in the Philippines. We have around 80 percent food that is cooked and comes from Spain.
PreviousNext

The Spanish brought their own cuisine with them and were influenced to the Filipino cuisine.  Some include corn, sausage, sapodilla, Spanish Beer 


Previous Next

The Philippines adapted many different customs and foods from Spanish colonizers. Spanish colonizers and friars in the 16th century brought with them produce from the New World such as  chili peppers, chocolate, corn, potatoes, tomatoes, pineapples, bell peppers, jicama, chayote, avocado,peanuts, annatto 


Previous Next

Hispanic dishes were eventually incorporated into Filipino cuisine with the more complex dishes usually being prepared for special occasions. Some dishes such as Arroz a la valenciana, Pochero, Ropa Vieja Chicken Pesa, Salad, Fish Sinigang, Bopes, Ambrosia Salad, Sopa Borracho
