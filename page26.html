SPANISH ERA: LANGUAGE & LITERATURE

About one-third (4,000) of the words in Tagalog are of Spanish origin, and around 6,000 words in other Filipino languages come from Spanish. During the Spanish period, literature flourished this time. Spanish language which became the literary language during this time lent many of its words to our language

Today it is estimated that about 20% of Tagalog words are Spanish. Considering the Philippines have cultural influences from China, Malaysia, Japan, and the United States, the fact that about a third of the words come from Spanish shows just how influential the Spanish Philippines era was on the culture.

In the Filibusterismo, he returns after reinventing himself as Simoun, the wealthy jeweler, and hastens social decay by further corrupting the social fabric till the oppressed react violently to overthrow the system.
