AMERICAN COLONIZATION

The Spanish-American war which started in Cuba, changed the history of the Philippines. Philippines was ceded to the United States by Spain in 1898 after a payment of $20 US to Spain in accordance with the Treaty of Paris ending the Spanish-American war that led with the American occupation to the Philippines. The period of American colonialization on the Philippines lasted for 48 years.
